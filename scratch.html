My Experience in China


I had the opprotunity a few years back to join a 3 week study tour/langauge learning experience across China. This trip presented a great chance to improve my Chinese and better understand the culture behind the language I was developing. This study abroad has fueled my desire to keep learning Mandarin and inspired me to join BYU's Chinese flagship program. At the end of this program I will be able to particiapte in a year long study abroad to Taiwan to study Information Systems from a Chinese perspective and solidify my Mandarin.

As part of our trip we visited several cities throughout China:

  1. Beijing
    • Capital of China
    • Home to the Forbidden Palace
    • Next to the Great Wall
  2. Chengdu
    • Capital of Sichuan province
    • While here, we studied for 10 days at Sichuan University
  3. Guilin
  4. Xi'an
    • Home to the Terracotta Army
  5. Yangshuo
  6. And finally back to Beijing
